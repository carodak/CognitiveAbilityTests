Experiment

Hello and welcome. I am your experimenter.

You will go through a set of exercises that will assess your abstract and spatial reasoning, your critical reasoning, your spatial orientation, your spatial memory and your working memory.

You will have to complete 5 sets of 4 exercises. At the start of each set of exercises, I will show you an example.

You will then be given time to complete the following exercises.

At the end of each exercise, you will need to indicate your level of confusion. The total duration of the experiment is approximately 30.
